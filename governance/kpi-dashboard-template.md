# KPI Dashboard Template

## Productivity KPIs
- slices completed per period
- average slice cycle time
- average review turnaround time
- average defect fix turnaround time

## Quality KPIs
- defects per completed slice
- % slices with revise outcome
- % tests with negative coverage
- open critical defects

## Flow KPIs
- requirement approval lead time
- architecture approval lead time
- implementation lead time
- testing lead time
- completion lead time
