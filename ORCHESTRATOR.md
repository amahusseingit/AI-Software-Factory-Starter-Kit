# ORCHESTRATOR.md

## Purpose
The Orchestrator is the process owner of the SDLC. It sequences, gates, hands off, tracks, and closes work.

## Responsibilities
1. Intake and kickoff
2. Requirements validation gate
3. Architecture readiness gate
4. Slice planning
5. Execution sequencing
6. Review gating
7. Testing oversight
8. Defect loop management
9. Approval coordination
10. Completion gate
